Hyderabad's Head Start

Hyderabad is not starting from zero. A strong foundation of existing smart city projects and traffic management infrastructure provides a robust launchpad for this next-generation system.

HTRIMS

The Hyderabad Traffic Integrated Management System already links 221 signals to a central command, using cameras for adaptive control and achieving a 32% speed increase.

TGICCC + RTA

The Telangana Integrated Command and Control Centre is integrated with the RTA database, enabling real-time vehicle data access for transparent enforcement.

GHMC Upgrades

The Greater Hyderabad Municipal Corporation is actively upgrading 400 signals to a city-wide smart Adaptive Traffic Signal Control (ATSC) system.

Telangana Police Tech

Utilizes AI for predictive policing and leverages a network of over 30,000 CCTV cameras for extensive city surveillance.

Transformative Benefits
